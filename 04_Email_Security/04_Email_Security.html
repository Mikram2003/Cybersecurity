Encryption

Protects email content from being read by unauthorized parties.

Example: SSL/TLS encryption.

Phishing Awareness

Trains users to recognize and avoid fraudulent emails.

Example: Recognizing suspicious links and attachments.

Spam Filters

Automatically detect and block unwanted and potentially harmful emails, helping keep your inbox clean and secure.

Example: Filtering out spam and junk emails.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

An additional security layer requiring users to provide two or more verification factors.

Example: Using a password and a code sent to your phone.
